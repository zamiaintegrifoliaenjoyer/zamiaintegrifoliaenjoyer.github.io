Hibiscus tiliaceus is a flowering tree native to the Eastern Hemisphere, around the Indian Ocean.

  • pollinated by several species of bees, including honey bees & carpenter bees (Aluri, et al., 2020)
  • used by the carpenter bee Xylocopa darwini to nest (Aluri, et al., 2020) and used by other insects to reproduce (Kohno and Bui Thi, 2005)
  • used in traditional medicine (Abdul-Awal, et al., 2016) and as a culinary ingredient (Lamanda, Malézieux, and Martin, 2006
  • its seeds drift through the ocean (Tang, et al., 2003)
